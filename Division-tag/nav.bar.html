Information About Computer

A compuert is programmable electronic device which accepts the data.

Computer history tracks the evolution of calculating and computing devices from ancient tools like the abacus to modern electronic machines, encompassing key developments such as mechanical calculators, programmable machines, vacuum tubes, transistors, and integrated circuits.

A computer is an electronic device capable of performing various tasks such as data processing, calculations, and operations that can be programmed to execute a wide range of applications. Computers process data according to instructions (called programs or software) to produce meaningful results.

A computer typically has two main categories of parts: hardware and software.
